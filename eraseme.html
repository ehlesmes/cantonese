Encapsulated Lesson Viewer

This entire layout is now managed by a single <lesson-viewer> component. The header and footer are internal to the component, while this text is injected via a <slot>.

Using slots allows the main page to focus on content while the component handles the layout constraints, such as keeping the header at the top and the footer at the bottom with a scrollable middle section.
