# Chapter 4: Identifying & Quantifying

This chapter introduces the fundamental building blocks of counting and identifying items in
Cantonese, centered around the essential concept of Classifiers (Measure Words).

## Lesson 4.1: Numbers 1-10 & The Rule of Two

- **Concepts:** Numbers 1-10. Understanding the difference between `ji6` (counting number) and
  `loeng5` (used with classifiers).
- **Examples:**
  - `jat1, ji6, saam1, sei3, ng5` (一, 二, 三, 四, 五) - 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
  - `loeng5` (兩) - Two (of something)
  - `luk6, cat1, baat3, gau2, sap6` (六, 七, 八, 九, 十) - 6, 7, 8, 9, 10
- **Exercises:**
  - `4.1.2` (Reading): `jat1, ji6, saam1`
  - `4.1.6` (Reading): `sei3, ng5, loeng5`
  - `4.1.3` (Unscramble): `jat1, ji6, saam1` sequence.
  - `4.1.4` (Reading): `luk6, cat1, baat3`
  - `4.1.7` (Reading): `gau2, sap6`
  - `4.1.5` (Unscramble): `baat3, gau2, sap6` sequence.

## Lesson 4.2: The Universal Classifier (go3)

- **Concepts:** The syntax of `Number + Classifier + Noun`. Introducing `go3` as the default measure
  word for people and general items.
- **Examples:**
  - `jat1 go3` (一個) - One (of)
  - `jat1 go3 jan4` (一個人) - One person
  - `loeng5 go3 hok6 saang1` (兩個學生) - Two students
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Counting People):**
    - A: `nei5 dei6 hai6 m4 hai6 saam1 go3 jan4 aa3?` (你哋係唔係三個人呀？)
    - B: `ngo5 dei6 saam1 go3 jan4.` (我哋三個人。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Counting Staff):**
    - A: `can4 ging1 lei5, ceng2 man6 gwai3 gung1 si1 jau5 gei2 do1 wai6 jyun4 gung1?`
      (陳經理，請問貴公司有幾多位員工？)
    - B: `ngo5 dei6 gung1 si1 jau5 sap6 wai6 jyun4 gung1.` (我哋公司有十位員工。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `4.2.2` (Reading): `jat1 go3 jan4`
  - `4.2.4` (Reading): `loeng5 go3 hok6 saang1`
  - `4.2.5` (Reading): `貴公司`
  - `4.2.6` (Reading): `員工`
  - `4.2.7` (Reading): `位`
  - `4.2.3` (Unscramble): `keoi5 dei6 hai6 saam1 go3 hok6 saang1`

## Lesson 4.3: Specifiers (This & That)

- **Concepts:** Using `ni1` (this) and `go2` (that) with classifiers to point out specific items.
- **Examples:**
  - `ni1 go3` (呢個) - This one
  - `go2 go3` (嗰個) - That one
  - `ni1 go3 jan4` (呢個人) - This person
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Pointing):**
    - A: `ni1 go3 hai6 m4 hai6 aa3 ming4 aa3?` (呢個係唔係阿明呀？)
    - B: `m4 hai6. go2 go3 sin1 hai6.` (唔係。嗰個先係。)
    - A: `o4, nei5 hou2, aa3 ming4.` (哦，你好，阿明。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Identifying Reports):**
    - A: `can4 ging1 lei5, ni1 fan6 bou3 gou3 hai6 mai6 go2 fan6?` (陳經理，呢份報告係咪嗰份？)
    - B: `hai6, go2 fan6 sin1 hai6.` (係，嗰份先係。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `4.3.2` (Reading): `ni1 go3 jan4` (Covers: 呢, 個, 人)
  - `4.3.4` (Reading): `go2 go3 hok6 saang1` (Covers: 嗰, 個, 學, 生)
  - `4.3.5` (Reading): `報告` (Covers: 報, 告)
  - `4.3.6` (Reading): `份` (Covers: 份)
  - `4.3.3` (Unscramble): `ni1 go3 hai6 m4 hai6 hok6 saang1 aa3?`
    (Covers: 呢, 個, 係, 唔, 學, 生, 呀)

## Lesson 4.4: Question Words (Which & How Many)

- **Concepts:** `bin1` (which) and `gei2 do1` (how many). Both require classifiers when referring to
  nouns.
- **Examples:**
  - `bin1 go3?` (邊個？) - Which one? / Who?
  - `gei2 do1 go3?` (幾多個？) - How many?
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Selection):**
    - A: `bin1 go3 hai6 sin1 saang1 aa3?` (邊個係先生呀？)
    - B: `ni1 go3.` (呢個。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Office Equipment):**
    - A: `can4 ging1 lei5, gwai3 gung1 si1 seoi1 jiu3 gei2 do1 bou6 din6 nou5?`
      (陳經理，貴公司需要幾多部電腦？)
    - B: `ngo5 dei6 seoi1 jiu3 ng5 bou6 din6 nou5.` (我哋需要五部電腦。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `4.4.2` (Reading): `bin1 go3?`
  - `4.4.4` (Reading): `gei2 do1 go3?`
  - `4.4.6` (Reading): `需要`
  - `4.4.7` (Reading): `電腦`
  - `4.4.8` (Reading): `部`
  - `4.4.5` (Unscramble): `gei2 do1 go3 jan4 aa3?`
  - `4.4.3` (Unscramble): `bin1 go3 hai6 sin1 saang1 aa3?`

## Lesson 4.5: Everyday Classifiers & Possessives

- **Concepts:** Introducing `zek3` (animals) and `bun2` (books). Possessive shortcut
  `Pronoun + Classifier + Noun`.
- **Examples:**
  - `zek3` (隻) - Classifier for animals.
  - `bun2` (本) - Classifier for books.
  - `ngo5 zek3 maau1` (我隻貓) - My cat (instead of `ngo5 ge3 maau1`).
  - `ni1 bun2 syu1` (呢本書) - This book.
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Informal - Possession):**
    - A: `ni1 bun2 hai6 m4 hai6 nei5 ge3 syu1 aa3?` (呢本係唔係你嘅書呀？)
    - B: `hai6 aa3. ni1 bun2 hai6 ngo5 bun2 syu1.` (係呀。呢本係我本書。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Borrowing Books):**
    - A: `can4 ging1 lei5, ngo5 ho2 ji5 ze3 ni1 loeng5 bun2 syu1 maa1?`
      (陳經理，我可以借呢兩本書嗎？)
    - B: `dong1 jin4 ho2 ji5, ni1 dou6 ceng2.` (當然可以，呢度請。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `4.5.2` (Reading): `zek3 maau1` (Covers: 隻, 貓)
  - `4.5.4` (Reading): `ni1 bun2 syu1` (Covers: 呢, 本, 書)
  - `4.5.5` (Reading): `ngo5 bun2 syu1` (Covers: 我, 本, 書)
  - `4.5.7` (Reading): `可以` (Covers: 可, 以)
  - `4.5.8` (Reading): `借` (Covers: 借)
  - `4.5.9` (Reading): `當然` (Covers: 當, 然)
  - `4.5.3` (Unscramble): `ni1 bun2 hai6 ngo5 bun2 syu1` (Covers: 呢, 本, 係, 我, 本, 書)
  - `4.5.6` (Unscramble): `go2 zek3 hai6 ngo5 zek3 maau1` (Covers: 嗰, 隻, 係, 我, 隻, 貓)
