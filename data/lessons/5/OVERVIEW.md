# Chapter 5: Context & Nuance

This chapter moves beyond simple identification and focuses on the "W-questions" (Where, When, How,
Why) and the expressive particles that add flavor and nuance to Cantonese speech.

## Lesson 5.1: Where? (Location & Placement)

- **Concepts:** Asking "where" (`邊度`) and stating locations using `喺` (at/in) and `去` (to go).
- **Examples:**
  - `bin1 dou6?` (邊度？) - Where?
  - `hai2` (喺) - At / In
  - `uk1 kei2` (屋企) - Home
  - `gung1 si1` (公司) - Office / Company
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Informal - Where):**
    - A: `nei5 hai2 bin1 dou6 aa3?` (你喺邊度呀？)
    - B: `ngo5 hai2 uk1 kei2. nei5 ne1?` (我喺屋企。你呢？)
    - A: `ngo5 hai2 gung1 si1.` (我喺公司。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Inquiring Location):**
    - A: `can4 ging1 lei5, ceng2 man6 nei5 ji4 gaa1 hai2 bin1 dou6?` (陳經理，請問你而家喺邊度？)
    - B: `ngo5 ji4 gaa1 hai2 baan6 gung1 sat1.` (我而家喺辦公室。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.1.2` (Reading): `邊度` (Covers: 邊, 度)
  - `5.1.5` (Reading): `而家` (Covers: 而, 家)
  - `5.1.6` (Reading): `辦公室` (Covers: 辦, 公, 室)
  - `5.1.3` (Unscramble): `nei5 hai2 bin1 dou6 aa3?` (Covers: 你, 喺, 邊, 度, 呀)
  - `5.1.4` (Unscramble): `ngo5 hai2 gung1 si1` (Covers: 我, 喺, 公, 司)

## Lesson 5.2: When? (Time & Dates)

- **Concepts:** Asking "when" (`幾時`) and common time-relative words.
- **Examples:**
  - `gei2 si4?` (幾時？) - When?
  - `gam1 jat6` (今日) - Today
  - `ting1 jat6` (聽日) - Tomorrow
  - `zoi3` (再) - Again / Then
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Informal - Plans):**
    - A: `nei5 gei2 si4 heoi3 gung1 si1 aa3?` (你幾時去公司呀？)
    - B: `ngo5 gam1 jat6 m4 heoi3. ngo5 ting1 jat6 sin1 heoi3.` (我今日唔去。我聽日先去。)
    - A: `hou2 aa3, ting1 jat6 gin3.` (好呀，聽日見。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Meeting Scheduling):**
    - A: `can4 ging1 lei5, wui2 ji5 gei2 si4 heoi2 hang4?` (陳經理，會議幾時舉行？)
    - B: `wui2 ji5 haa6 sing1 kei4 heoi2 hang4.` (會議下星期舉行。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.2.2` (Reading): `今日，聽日` (Covers: 今, 日, 聽, 日)
  - `5.2.5` (Reading): `會議` (Covers: 會, 議)
  - `5.2.6` (Reading): `下星期` (Covers: 下, 星, 期)
  - `5.2.3` (Unscramble): `幾時去呀？` (Covers: 幾, 時, 去, 呀)
  - `5.2.4` (Unscramble): `今日我去公司` (Covers: 今, 日, 我, 去, 公, 司)

## Lesson 5.3: How? (Manner & Method)

- **Concepts:** Asking "how" (`點樣`) and common transit/action methods.
- **Examples:**
  - `dim2 joeng2?` (點樣？) - How?
  - `daap3 ce1` (搭車) - To take a car/bus
  - `haang4 lou6` (行路) - To walk
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Informal - Travel):**
    - A: `nei5 dim2 joeng2 heoi3 gung1 si1 aa3?` (你點樣去公司呀？)
    - B: `ngo5 daap3 ce1. nei5 ne1?` (我搭車。你呢？)
    - A: `ngo5 haang4 lou6.` (我行路。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Business Travel):**
    - A:
      `can4 ging1 lei5, ceng2 man6 gwai3 gung1 si1 tung4 si6 hai6 dim2 joeng2 heoi3 wui2 ji5 ge3?`
      (陳經理，請問貴公司同事係點樣去會議嘅？)
    - B: `keoi5 dei6 daap3 dei6 tit3 heoi3.` (佢哋搭地鐵去。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.3.2` (Reading): `搭車，行路` (Covers: 搭, 車, 行, 路)
  - `5.3.5` (Reading): `同事` (Covers: 同, 事)
  - `5.3.6` (Reading): `地鐵` (Covers: 地, 鐵)
  - `5.3.7` (Reading): `行` (Covers: 行)
  - `5.3.8` (Reading): `路` (Covers: 路)
  - `5.3.9` (Reading): `搭` (Covers: 搭)
  - `5.3.10` (Reading): `車` (Covers: 車)
  - `5.3.3` (Unscramble): `點樣去呀？` (Covers: 點, 樣, 去, 呀)
  - `5.3.4` (Unscramble): `我搭車去` (Covers: 我, 搭, 車, 去)

## Lesson 5.4: Why? (Cause & Effect)

- **Concepts:** Asking "why" (`點解`) and answering with `因為` (because).
- **Examples:**
  - `dim2 gaai2?` (點解？) - Why?
  - `jan1 wai6` (因為) - Because
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Informal - Work):**
    - A: `nei5 dim2 gaai2 gam1 jat6 m4 heoi3 gung1 si1 aa3?` (你點解今日唔去公司呀？)
    - B: `jan1 wai6 ngo5 gam1 jat6 hai2 uk1 kei2 zou6 je5.` (因為我今日喺屋企做嘢。)
    - A: `o4, hou2 aa3.` (哦，好呀。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Project Delay):**
    - A: `can4 ging1 lei5, dim2 gaai2 hong6 muk6 wui5 jin4 kei4?` (陳經理，點解項目會延期？)
    - B: `jan1 wai6 jan4 sau2 bat1 zuk1.` (因為人手不足。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.4.2` (Reading): `因為...` (Covers: 因, 為)
  - `5.4.5` (Reading): `項目` (Covers: 項, 目)
  - `5.4.6` (Reading): `人手` (Covers: 人, 手)
  - `5.4.7` (Reading): `因` (Covers: 因)
  - `5.4.8` (Reading): `為` (Covers: 為)
  - `5.4.3` (Unscramble): `點解唔去呀？` (Covers: 點, 解, 唔, 去, 呀)
  - `5.4.4` (Unscramble): `因為我喺屋企` (Covers: 因, 為, 我, 喺, 屋, 企)

## Lesson 5.5: Expressive Particles (Flavoring)

- **Concepts:** Adding nuance to statements with advanced particles.
- **Examples:**
  - `zaa3` (咋) - Only / Just (limiting)
  - `gwaa3` (啩) - I suppose / Probably (uncertainty)
  - `bo3` (噃) - Realization / Mild warning
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Informal - Uncertainty):**
    - A: `keoi5 dei6 gei2 do1 go3 jan4 heoi3 aa3?` (佢哋幾多個人去呀？)
    - B: `saam1 go3 jan4 zaa3.` (三個人咋。)
    - A: `hai6 gwaa3.` (係啩。)
  - **Dialogue 2 (Formal - Business Report):**
    - A: `can4 ging1 lei5, geoi3 sik1 bun2 jyut6 jip6 zik1 zoeng1 wui5 zang1 zoeng2.`
      (陳經理，據悉本月業績將會增長。)
    - B: `hai6, kok3 hai6 jyu4 ci2.` (係，確係如此。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.5.2` (Reading): `兩個人咋` (Covers: 兩, 個, 人)
  - `5.5.6` (Reading): `業績` (Covers: 業, 績)
  - `5.5.7` (Reading): `增長` (Covers: 增, 長)
  - `5.5.8` (Reading): `噃` (Covers: 噃)
  - `5.5.3` (Unscramble): `佢喺屋企啩` (Covers: 佢, 喺, 屋, 企, 啩)
  - `5.5.4` (Unscramble): `三個人咋` (Covers: 三, 個, 人)
  - `5.5.5` (Unscramble): `好噃` (Covers: 好, 噃)
  - `5.5.5` (Unscramble): `好大噃`
