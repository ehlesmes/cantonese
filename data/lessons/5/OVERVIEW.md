# Chapter 5: Context & Nuance

This chapter moves beyond simple identification and focuses on the "W-questions" (Where, When, How, Why) and the expressive particles that add flavor and nuance to Cantonese speech.

## Lesson 5.1: Where? (Location & Placement)

- **Concepts:** Asking "where" (`邊度`) and stating locations using `喺` (at/in) and `去` (to go).
- **Examples:**
  - `bin1 dou6?` (邊度？) - Where?
  - `hai2` (喺) - At / In
  - `uk1 kei2` (屋企) - Home
  - `gung1 si1` (公司) - Office / Company
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Neutral/Formal):**
    - A: `nei5 hai2 bin1 dou6 aa3?` (你喺邊度呀？)
    - B: `ngo5 hai2 uk1 kei2. nei5 ne1?` (我喺屋企。你呢？)
    - A: `ngo5 hai2 gung1 si1.` (我喺公司。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.1.2` (Reading): `喺屋企，去公司`
  - `5.1.3` (Unscramble): `你喺邊度呀？`
  - `5.1.4` (Unscramble): `我喺屋企`

## Lesson 5.2: When? (Time & Dates)

- **Concepts:** Asking "when" (`幾時`) and common time-relative words.
- **Examples:**
  - `gei2 si4?` (幾時？) - When?
  - `gam1 jat6` (今日) - Today
  - `ting1 jat6` (聽日) - Tomorrow
  - `zoi3` (再) - Again / Then
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Neutral/Formal):**
    - A: `nei5 gei2 si4 heoi3 gung1 si1 aa3?` (你幾時去公司呀？)
    - B: `ngo5 gam1 jat6 m4 heoi3. ngo5 ting1 jat6 sin1 heoi3.` (我今日唔去。我聽日先去。)
    - A: `hou2 aa3, ting1 jat6 gin3.` (好呀，聽日見。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.2.2` (Reading): `今日，聽日`
  - `5.2.3` (Unscramble): `幾時去呀？`
  - `5.2.4` (Unscramble): `今日我去公司`

## Lesson 5.3: How? (Manner & Method)

- **Concepts:** Asking "how" (`點樣`) and common transit/action methods.
- **Examples:**
  - `dim2 joeng2?` (點樣？) - How?
  - `daap3 ce1` (搭車) - To take a car/bus
  - `haang4 lou6` (行路) - To walk
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Neutral/Formal):**
    - A: `nei5 dim2 joeng2 heoi3 gung1 si1 aa3?` (你點樣去公司呀？)
    - B: `ngo5 daap3 ce1. nei5 ne1?` (我搭車。你呢？)
    - A: `ngo5 haang4 lou6.` (我行路。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.3.2` (Reading): `搭車，行路`
  - `5.3.3` (Unscramble): `點樣去呀？`
  - `5.3.4` (Unscramble): `我搭車去`

## Lesson 5.4: Why? (Cause & Effect)

- **Concepts:** Asking "why" (`點解`) and answering with `因為` (because).
- **Examples:**
  - `dim2 gaai2?` (點解？) - Why?
  - `jan1 wai6` (因為) - Because
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Neutral/Formal):**
    - A: `nei5 dim2 gaai2 gam1 jat6 m4 heoi3 gung1 si1 aa3?` (你點解今日唔去公司呀？)
    - B: `jan1 wai6 ngo5 gam1 jat6 hai2 uk1 kei2 zou6 je5.` (因為我今日喺屋企做嘢。)
    - A: `o4, hou2 aa3.` (哦，好呀。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.4.2` (Reading): `因為...`
  - `5.4.3` (Unscramble): `點解唔去呀？`
  - `5.4.4` (Unscramble): `因為我喺屋企`

## Lesson 5.5: Expressive Particles (Flavoring)

- **Concepts:** Adding nuance to statements with advanced particles.
- **Examples:**
  - `zaa3` (咋) - Only / Just (limiting)
  - `gwaa3` (啩) - I suppose / Probably (uncertainty)
  - `bo3` (噃) - Realization / Mild warning
- **Dialogues:**
  - **Dialogue 1 (Neutral/Informal):**
    - A: `keoi5 dei6 gei2 do1 go3 jan4 heoi3 aa3?` (佢哋幾多個人去呀？)
    - B: `saam1 go3 jan4 zaa3.` (三個人咋。)
    - A: `hai6 gwaa3.` (係啩。)
- **Exercises:**
  - `5.5.2` (Reading): `兩個人咋`
  - `5.5.3` (Unscramble): `佢喺屋企啩`
  - `5.5.4` (Unscramble): `三個人咋`
  - `5.5.5` (Unscramble): `好大噃`
