# A1 Cantonese Curriculum Map

Target: Basic communication skills. Focus on listening, reading, and common daily scenarios.

## Lessons
- **Lesson 1: Introductions**
  - Goal: Basic greetings, asking/telling names.
  - Grammar: Subject-Verb-Object, particles (e.g., 嘅, 呢).
  - Vocab: Personal pronouns, common greetings, basic "to be" / "to call".
- **Lesson 2: Daily Needs**
  - Goal: Shopping and simple food ordering.
  - Grammar: Quantity and numbers, "I want" construction (想要).
  - Vocab: Numbers, food items, currency, "how much".
- **Lesson 3: Time & Routines**
  - Goal: Discussing the time and daily schedule.
  - Grammar: Time indicators (早, 晏, 晚), frequency (成日, 有時).
  - Vocab: Days, hours, common verbs for routine.
