# A1 Cantonese Curriculum

Target: Basic communication skills. Focus on listening, reading, and common daily scenarios.

- **Lesson 1: Introductions**
  - Goal: Basic greetings, asking/telling names.
- **Lesson 2: Daily Needs**
  - Goal: Shopping and simple food ordering.
- **Lesson 3: Time & Routines**
  - Goal: Discussing the time and daily schedule.
